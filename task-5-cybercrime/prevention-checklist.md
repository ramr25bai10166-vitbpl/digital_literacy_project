\# Checklist for keeping you safe online

1. Use a strong password or biometric authentication to lock apps on your device. 
2. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking applications. 
3. Do not share your One-Time Password (OTP), UPI PIN, or any other paswords with anyone for any reason, even if they say they are from your bank. 
4. If someone asks you to send them money through a QR code, do NOT do so, as only the sender from an application can receive payment through a QR code. 
5. Do not access sensitive accounts or bank accounts using public Wi-Fi networks. 
6. Check application permissions. If you see that the application has unnecessary permissions, remove them from the application. 
7. Check links before you click on them, especially if they are from an unknown e-mail or message. 
8. Make sure to update both your phone's and all of the applications to the latest version in order to fix security vulnerabelities. 
9. Log-out of any shared equipment (i.e. school computers) once you are done using it.
